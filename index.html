EMILY ROSE

Emily was born in West London in 1986. She trained at The Arts Educational School in Chiswick finding her passion for art and painting. Following this she received her diploma in Fine Art at London Chelsea College of Art and Design.

 

Since graduating with a BA Hons in Film & Theatre she has continued as a self-taught fine artist painting mainly for family and friends and receiving her first commissioned artwork at the end of 2016.

 

Emily's commissions are all done to order to meet individual clients needs. Working mainly in oils on canvas, capturing special events in your lives such as weddings, cars, portraits and other treasured memories.

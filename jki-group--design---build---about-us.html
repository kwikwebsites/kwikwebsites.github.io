ABOUT US

The founder, Jay Krastev, has over 16 years’ experience in the industry, where he has successfully delivered some renowned projects and won a number of loyal clients  who use his services time and time again.

 

Because our clients range from restaurant owners to hospitals, we understand the unique requirements and complexities that each project brings.  We pride ourselves on our flexibility and our belief that anything is possible to make our clients’ dreams a reality.

 

We can always be relied on to deliver on time and budget, which is why so many businesses choose JKI Group.
